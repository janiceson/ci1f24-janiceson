Suspect Profiles

George Hodel

A prominent doctor in Los Angeles, George Hodel was one of the primary suspects in the Black Dahlia case due to his proximity and behavior, which raised suspicions.

Mark Hansen

A nightclub owner acquainted with Elizabeth Short. He was questioned due to his connection to her, but there was no concrete evidence tying him to the crime.

Leslie Dillon

Former mortician's assistant with a deep knowledge of anatomy. Dillon became a suspect due to his peculiar fascination with the case details.
